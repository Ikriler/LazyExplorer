Cloud Overview

Last Updated: October 21, 2017

Wildfire Cloud is an online service created and maintained by the Wildfire Team that allows you to use online-only services and run simulations online without the need to use your local machine.

Getting Set Up

To use the Wildfire Cloud, you must first sign up for an account at https://cloud.wildfire.ai/register/.

Once you have signed up, you'll be at the Wildfire Cloud dashboard. You should click the Register Extension button in the top-right corner in order to get your extension associated with your account.

You can tell if you're registered if the button is green and says Open Extension. Now let's look at the upload functionality now available in the Workflow Editor.

Uploading and Simulating

You'll now have a new option in your Workflow Editor toolbar. Clicking the Upload to Wildfire Cloud button will prompt you to provide a name for your workflow when it is stored in the Wildfire Cloud.

Choose an appropriate name for your workflow upload and click OK. Your workflow is now uploaded so let's head over to your My Workflows section.

This is where you can view all your previously uploaded workflows. Click on your workflows image, or click the View Details button to proceed.

This is where you can review all simulations of your workflow. You can simply click the Queue Simulation button to begin. This will place your simulation into a queue which may take anywhere between a few seconds to a few minutes to process, depending upon the demand at the time.

Your queued simulation will show you when it is Queued, Running or Finished. When it is finished, you can click the View button to view a video of your simulation and review the logs it generated.
